Working principles

Understanding is built, not collected.

These notes are not a warehouse of definitions. They are an attempt to reconstruct each idea from its assumptions, test it against examples, and connect symbols back to something we can see.

  1. 01

    Begin with the question.

    A formula matters only after we know what problem forced it to exist.

  2. 02

    Move between views.

    Algebra, geometry, computation, and intuition should explain one another.

  3. 03

    Leave the trail visible.

    Confusion, revisions, and failed attempts are part of the learning record.
